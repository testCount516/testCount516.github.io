About St. Johns
Electric

When looking for an electrician in the Nassau County and the New York Metropolitan Area, look no further than the experts at St. Johns Electric. Run by a management team with over 40 years of combined electrical experience, St. Johns Electric has been dedicated to providing expert electrical service since 1987. We handle everything from remodel jobs, electrical upgrades and regular maintenence, to installing security systems, video surveillance, and telecom/computer-network cabling.

Our Mission is to provide prompt, reliable electrical service and repair with the highest level of professionalism, courtesy, knowledge, and quality workmanship in order to guarantee 100% customer satisfaction and build lasting relationships with all of our customers.

Our Services

When it comes to electrical services we do it all!
For a sample of some of our most popular services, look below.

Residential & Commercial Solutions

Our electricians specialize in residential & commercial service and repair including diagnostic trouble-shooting, adding outdoor flood lights, smoke detectors, child proof outlets, ceiling fans, service changes, electrical remodel work, and more.

Emergency & Backup Services

We understand how stressful electrical emergencies are, which is why we offer our clients 24-7 service when it comes to emergency outages. And those looking to ensure their business or house will stay operational even in citywide outages, our team can engineer a backup system to ensure your essentials are always up and running,

Telecom & Computer Networks

Staying connected is a must for everyone these days -- businesses and households alike. St. John's Electric are experts when it comes to network and telecommunications wiring, making sure that your business and home are always online and available.

Surveillance & Security Systems

Whether you have an inventory to keep safe or a home to protect, electronic monitoring systems -- proven deterent against thieves -- will not only improve security, but they can offer a peace of mind for businesses and homeowners alike. That's why we at St. Johns Eletric offers full security system installation and maintenance.
